Chapter 01 — Burnout
—

Designers carry the most.

Designers report the highest burnout of any discipline. The gap tracks with always-on client work and endless revision cycles, well ahead of writers and strategists.

Chapter 02 — The tools
—

AI moved in. It didn't take over.

A clear majority of creatives use AI tools weekly, but almost none let it near the final craft. Most use it for first drafts and admin, then do the real work by hand.

Chapter 03 — Money

Seniority beats geography.

Typical day rate climbs steeply with seniority, from junior level to director. Where you live matters far less than what you do and how senior you are.

Chapter 04 — Where we work
—

Hybrid quietly won.

Most creatives now work hybrid, with a large remote minority and only a small share back in an office full time. The ones most likely to be in are under 25, looking for mentorship.
